Medical Schemes

National Health Insurance Scheme

This scheme provides cover to all citizens of the nation for accidents or natural disasters that cause disability or death, or for disability due to a household's primary or sole breadwinner.

Central Government Health Scheme

The Central Government Health Scheme provides health facilities to citizens enlisted under the scheme. It provides coverage and cashless facilities to enrolled members. Government employees are eligible for this coverage.

Ayushman Bharat Yojana

The Ayushman Bharat Jan Arogya Yojana is the world's largest publicly funded health insurance scheme. It provides free treatment up to Rs. 5 lakh per family per year for secondary and tertiary health services to over 13 crore families.

Universal Health Insurance Scheme

The Universal Health Insurance Scheme provides coverage for all medical expenses, including cover for personal accident of the family's primary breadwinner. Compensation is also provided in case of the primary breadwinner's death.
