Gaurima Shelter Home

Gaurima Home aims to provide shelter, food, medical care, and entertainment facilities to homeless and abandoned transgender individuals. The comprehensive schemes include providing shelter with basic facilities such as food, clothing, entertainment, medical facilities, and counseling.

Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana

The scheme allows for a maximum annual deposit of Rs. 1.5 lakh. It requires investment for up to 14 years. Upon maturity, the amount will be given to your daughter. The amount deposited and the benefits received on maturity are eligible for tax exemption under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act.

Annapurna Scheme

The goal of the Annapurna Scheme is to fulfill the needs of those elderly citizens who, despite being eligible, have not benefited from the National Old Age Pension Scheme (NOAPS) by providing food security. Beneficiaries of the Annapurna Scheme are provided with 10 kg of grains "free" every month.
